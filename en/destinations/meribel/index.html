Méribel: the most British resort in the Alps

Sitting at the geographic heart of the world's largest ski area, Méribel combines authentic Savoyard architecture with exceptional accessibility and the deepest British demand base of any French resort.

The heart of the 3 Vallées

Méribel occupies the central valley of the 3 Vallées, giving skiers direct access to 600 km of pistes across Courchevel, Val Thorens, and the Tarentaise valleys. This central position is Méribel's most powerful selling point: guests can ski to different resort centres each day without returning to the same terrain. For property investors, this means Méribel attracts the widest range of international guests — from beginners to experts — sustaining demand across the full season.

British heartland since the 1930s

Méribel was effectively founded by Peter Lindsay, a British diplomat who fell in love with the valley in 1938. The British connection has deepened ever since. Today, British visitors represent the majority of international guests, and the resort's social scene, chalet culture, and après-ski atmosphere mirror British expectations closely. This creates exceptionally reliable rental demand: British families rebook year after year, and premium weeks (Christmas, New Year, February half-term) consistently sell out months in advance.

Authentic architecture, premium values

Unlike purpose-built resorts, Méribel enforces strict architectural rules that preserve the traditional Savoyard aesthetic: stone, wood, and chalet proportions dominate the skyline. This commitment to authenticity has protected and enhanced property values — Méribel properties have appreciated significantly in recent years and continue to command a premium over comparable ski properties elsewhere in the Alps.

Questions & answers

Frequently asked questions

How does the guaranteed rent work in Méribel?

ALPÉON signs a multi-year commercial lease directly with you and pays a fixed monthly rent regardless of actual occupancy. You receive your income whether the chalet is full or empty — no management fees, no empty-week deductions.

What rental yield can I expect in Méribel?

Net yields typically range from 4% to 6.5% of property value per year. Méribel's combination of 3 Vallées access, British demand depth, and authentic architecture supports strong absolute rent figures throughout the season.

I'm a UK resident — how is rental income from Méribel taxed?

Income from French property is taxed in France under the Franco-British double tax treaty. UK residents declare the income in both countries but receive a tax credit in the UK to avoid double taxation. We recommend consulting a cross-border tax specialist.

Why is Méribel so popular with British guests?

Méribel has been a British favourite since it was founded in the 1930s by a British diplomat. The resort has retained strong cultural ties with the UK, and the chalet-style hospitality, English-speaking staff, and British après-ski culture make it feel familiar and welcoming to British guests. This creates the deepest and most reliable British rental demand of any French resort.

What weekly rates does my Méribel property achieve?

Weekly rates in Méribel range from around €4,000 for a one-bed apartment to over €30,000 for a luxury chalet. Méribel-Mottaret properties command a 15–25% premium over equivalent properties lower in the resort due to direct piste access and higher altitude snow security.

What is the difference between Méribel Village and Méribel-Mottaret?

Méribel Village (1,450 m) is the main resort with a traditional Savoyard character, lively après-ski scene, and most restaurants and shops. Méribel-Mottaret (1,750 m) sits higher with fully ski-in/ski-out access to the 3 Vallées gondola and is better suited to guests who prioritise snow security and slope access. Both command strong rental rates, with Mottaret attracting a slightly more ski-focused clientele willing to pay a premium for the altitude.

How quickly can I start receiving guaranteed rent?

Once we visit your property and agree on the guaranteed rent amount, the lease can be signed within 2 weeks and your first payment received within 30 days of signing the contract.
